What's in store

BOARDGAMES

Boardgames are available all over the place; what we offer is a selection of games you won't find at big box stores. Boardgames are experiencing a resurgence in popularity that has led to thousands of new titles being released in recent years. We will help you find the games that fit your personality.

ROLE PLAYING GAMES

Role Playing games allow for interactive story telling in a way that really can't be matched by any other media. In most cases a game master (GM) will choose a setting (fantasy adventure, Super Hero, space flight, really anything you can imagine); then the GM and players will create the story together.

SCIENCE FICTION NOVELS

Science Fiction comes in many shapes and sizes, from dystopian futures portrayed in books like 1984 (George Orwell) and The Hunger Games (Suzanne Collins) to hard science fiction grounded in reality like The Martian (Andy Weir) and 2001: A Space Odyssey (Arthur C. Clarke). We stock a variety to please all audiences.

FANTASY NOVELS

Fantasy novels can take you away from the real world unlike anything else. From the classic authors like J.R.R. Tolkien and Ursula K. LeGuin to newer authors like Nnedi Okorafor and Tui T. Southerland that you may not have heard of yet, we strive to keep a great selection available.

ART BOOKS

We keep and are expanding a collection of Sci-fi and Fantasy art books. These beautiful large scale books create great living room centerpieces to spark the imagination or start conversation.

TAROT

We have a selection of Tarot and Oracle decks in stock and are always on the lookout for more unique and special decks.
